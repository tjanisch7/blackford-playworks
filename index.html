Unleash the Power of Play!

The mission of Playworks at Blackford Elementary is to provide a safe, fun, and inclusive environment for students during recess. In partnership with teachers, principals, and parents, we build a culture of play that enables kids to feel a real sense of belonging and have the opportunity to contribute on the playground, in the classroom, and into their communities.

Junior Coaches



The junior coach program is both and in-school and an after school program that aims to teach leadership skills to students that they can apply not only on the playground, but in the classroom as well. Skills such as problem solving, conflict resolution, and cooperation are taught through the use of lesson activities and scenarios on the playground. During recess, junior coaches assist the Playworks coach in maintaining the healthy play atmosphere that both Blackford and Playworks promote.










Recess Games


At Blackford, there are a variety of games being played on the playground throughout the day. All of these games promote the concept of inclusion, stressing that everyone, regardless of skill, is welcome to play.

Kickball

Kickball is without a doubt one of the most popular and requested games to play during recess at Blackford. It is uncommon for there to be a small amount of students playing at recess/lunch.
